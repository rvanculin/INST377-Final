SunTrail

Suntrail is built to work on all devices and web browsers. It is a lightweight web application that calculates the sunrise and sunset times at your given coordinates. Built with hikers, travlers, and campers in mind giving you a fast and simple website that can give you the information you need even with limited bandwith. Allowing you to plan ahead and check sunrise and sunset data in their exact location.
